Become a SHPE National Member!

OneSHPE used to be the old system to register as a member, but it is now deprecated.
To become a SHPE National member, you must create an account on SHPE Connect.

Click Me to Start the Application.
After clicking 'Join Now', follow the steps under 'Not a Registered User Yet?' if you haven't
created an account yet. Make sure you select Region 2, under the UC Riverside chapter to become associated
with SHPE-UCR! For both new members and old members who were last registered over a year ago, you must
create/renew your membership along with a small payment. More details are shown once you register.
